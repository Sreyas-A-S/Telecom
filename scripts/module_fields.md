# User Guide: Required Information for Modules

This document outlines the data and information required for each module in the system.

## 1. Employees
Information needed to register or update an employee profile.

*   **Name**: Full name of the employee.
*   **Email**: Official email address (used for login).
*   **Mobile**: Contact number.
*   **Gender**: Male, Female, or Other.
*   **Date of Birth**: Employee's birth date.
*   **Address**: Residential address.
*   **Country**: Country of residence.
*   **Profile Picture**: Photo of the employee.
*   **Marital Status**: Single, Married, etc.
*   **Father's Name**: Name of the father.
*   **Mother's Name**: Name of the mother.
*   **Spouse's Name**: Name of the spouse (if applicable).
*   **Blood Group**: Blood type (e.g., A+, O-).
*   **Emergency Contact**: Emergency contact number.
*   **Shirt Size**: Size of the formal shirt.
*   **T-Shirt Size**: Size of the t-shirt.
*   **Aadhar No**: Aadhar card number.
*   **PAN No**: PAN card number.
*   **Employee ID**: Unique identification number.
*   **Designation**: Job title (e.g., Manager, Sales Executive).
*   **Department**: The department they belong to.
*   **Role**: System access role (e.g., Admin, User).
*   **Joining Date**: Date of joining the company.
*   **Reporting To**: The manager or supervisor they report to.
*   **Is Broker**: Indicates if the employee is a broker.
*   **Task Elapsed Time**: Time spent on tasks.
*   **Bank Name**: Name of the bank.
*   **Account Number**: Bank account number.
*   **IFSC Code**: Bank branch IFSC code.
*   **Branch**: Bank branch name.
*   **PF No**: Provident Fund number.
*   **ESI No**: Employee State Insurance number.
*   **LWF No**: Labour Welfare Fund number.
*   **Dealership**: The dealership they are assigned to.
*   **Zone**: The geographical zone they operate in.

## 2. Parts
Information needed to add a new spare part to the inventory.

*   **Part Number**: Unique code for the part.
*   **Material Description**: Name or description of the part.
*   **HSN Code**: Harmonized System of Nomenclature code.
*   **Associated Product**: The product this part belongs to.
*   **Unit Price**: Cost per unit.
*   **Tax Category**: Applicable tax rate.
*   **Stock Quantity**: Current number of items in stock.
*   **Bin/Location**: Physical storage location (Bin number).
*   **Dealer**: The dealer associated with this part (Name/String).
*   **Dealership**: The dealership associated with this part.
*   **Active Status**: Whether the part is currently active/available.

## 3. Products
Information needed to list a new product.

*   **Name**: Name of the product.
*   **Description**: Detailed description of the product.
*   **HSN/SAC Code**: Tax classification code.
*   **Brochure**: Link or file path to the product brochure.
*   **Category**: Main category (e.g., Electronics, Spares).
*   **Sub Category**: Specific sub-group.
*   **Price**: Selling price (High precision).
*   **Unit Type**: Unit of measurement (e.g., pcs, kg, ltr).
*   **Tax Category**: Applicable tax bracket.

## 4. Clients
Information needed to register a client or lead.

*   **Name**: Client's full name (including Salutation like Mr./Ms.).
*   **Email**: Email address.
*   **Phone Number**: Contact number.
*   **Address**: Physical address.
*   **Profile Picture**: Photo of the client.
*   **GPS Location**: GPS coordinates string.
*   **Latitude**: Latitude coordinate.
*   **Longitude**: Longitude coordinate.
*   **State**: State of residence.
*   **District**: District of residence.
*   **Lead Source**: Where the lead came from (e.g., Website, Referral).
*   **Lead Category**: Category of the lead (e.g., Hot, Cold, New).
*   **Lead ID**: Associated Lead ID.
*   **Dealership**: The dealership handling this client.
*   **Assigned Employee**: The staff member responsible for this client.
*   **Agent Details**: Associated agent or agent type (if applicable).
*   **Notes**: Any special remarks or notes about the client.

## 5. Leads / Pipeline
Information needed to create and manage sales leads.

*   **Name**: Name of the lead contact.
*   **Salutation**: Title (e.g., Mr., Ms., Dr.).
*   **Email**: Email address.
*   **Phone Number**: Contact number.
*   **Location**: Address or location description.
*   **Map Location**: Google Maps link or description.
*   **Latitude**: GPS Latitude.
*   **Longitude**: GPS Longitude.
*   **Client**: Existing client associated with this lead.
*   **Dealership**: The dealership handling this lead.
*   **Employee**: The staff member assigned to this lead.
*   **Agent Details**: Associated agent or agent type.
*   **Lead Value**: Estimated monetary value of the lead.
*   **Status**: Current status (e.g., Pending, Won, Lost).
*   **Chance of Success**: Probability of closing (percentage).
*   **Product**: Interested product.
*   **Product Model**: Specific model of the product.
*   **Model Series**: Series of the product model.
*   **Quantity**: Number of units required.
*   **Financier**: Name of the financier (if applicable).
*   **Type**: Type of lead.
*   **Login Status**: Status of the login (if applicable).
*   **Stage**: Current stage in the sales pipeline.
*   **Billing**: Billing status or details.
*   **Remarks**: Additional notes or comments.
*   **Lead Source**: Source of the lead (e.g., Campaign, Walk-in).
*   **Lead Category**: Category of the lead.
*   **Last Status Before Conversion**: Status prior to conversion.
*   **Allow Follow Up**: Whether follow-ups are permitted.

## 6. Services
Information needed to schedule and manage service requests.

*   **Name**: Title or name of the service request.
*   **Description**: Detailed description of the issue or service needed.
*   **Client**: The client requesting the service.
*   **Product**: The product requiring service.
*   **Product Model**: Specific model of the product.
*   **Model Series**: Series of the product model.
*   **Dealership**: The dealership handling the service.
*   **Service Engineer**: Primary engineer assigned.
*   **Second Service Engineer**: Secondary engineer assigned (if any).
*   **Requested Location**: Location where service is required.
*   **Referral ID**: Unique referral identifier.
*   **Price**: Cost of the service.
*   **Due Date 1**: First deadline or scheduled date.
*   **Due Date 2**: Second deadline or follow-up date.
*   **Machine Status**: Current status of the machine.
*   **Type of Service**: Type of service (e.g., Repair, Maintenance).
*   **Contact Info**: Contact details for the service.
*   **Latitude**: GPS Latitude of service location.
*   **Longitude**: GPS Longitude of service location.
